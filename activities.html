Birth day parties

celebrate birthdays with us

get the chance to celebrae a out of the box birthday party with us at our court yard.Take your birthday party out of hotels or homes and join with the nature. Children can enjoy the beautiful environment of Dehiwala Zoological Gardens. Holding a party at Colombo zoo is easy.
Two Birthday Party Packages are available:

  • park with animals LKR 10000 (guinea pigs,chimpanzee,cockatoo,horses)
  • park without animals LKR 5000

you are allowed to bring your own refreshments or you can buy refreshments from our stalls
available from 8am-5pm
